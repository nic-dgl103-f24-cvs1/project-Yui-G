kehler vegetable company

Located in the heart of Black Creek, our small family farm is growing great things for our local community.

What we do

Kehler Vegetable Company is a small mixed market farm of vegetables, berries and eggs from our pasture located on a 20 acre farm in the Comox Valley.
The farmstand runs on the honour system and is stocked up daily right at the farm gate!

About us

We are family-owned and operated farm nestled in the heart of Black Creek. We take pride in the generations of hard work and dedication that have shaped our land into a thriving and sustainable farm.
